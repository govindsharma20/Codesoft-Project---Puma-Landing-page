About Us

Puma is a renowned global sportswear brand, recognized for its high-quality athletic and casual footwear, apparel, and accessories. Founded in 1948 by Rudolf Dassler in Herzogenaurach, Germany, Puma has grown to become one of the leading sports brands in the world. The brand is known for its innovative designs, performance-enhancing technology, and stylish aesthetics that cater to both professional athletes and everyday consumers.
